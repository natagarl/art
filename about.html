My name is Natasha Garlukovich (full name - Natallia Garlukovich). I’m a belarusian artist and illustrator.

I’ve got a degree in design in Belarusian State University (2010 - 2015). Then I fall in love with illustration. Now I still draw trying to translate ideas that I think are relevant at the moment.

I usually work digitaly using iPad with Apple Pencil and Procreate app.

I currently live in Warsaw, Poland with my husband and a cat.
