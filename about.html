My name is Natasha Garlukovich and I am a Belarusian artist based in Warsaw, Poland. I create illustrations and paintings.

I’ve got a degree in interior design in Belarusian State University. Then I became interested in illustration, started to grow in it and create something. Mostly I have been drawing digitally, but now my interest is shifting to traditional mediums.

Creativity itself brings me great joy. I focus on personal projects, share the pieces I have created, and hope that my works will bring something good to you too.
