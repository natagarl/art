Diary

August - November 2020

Hello!

There is so much going on.

The coronavirus doesn't seem so scary anymore.

But one day everything will be fine.

There is a dream.

Creativity.

Inspiration.

But someone does not understand.

People are like ears under your sickle...

"Shchuchynshchyna" is like a cure for sadness.

Fall.

And I grieve with everyone.

Where it's safe.

But hope lives on.
